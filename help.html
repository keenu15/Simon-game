Rules of the game

  • Press any key to start the game. Simon will give the first signal.Repeat the signal by clicking the same colored square.
  • Simon will give the next signal. Repeat the previous signal and the new signal, in order.
  • Simon will add another signal.You have to repeat the previous signals and the new signal, in order.
  • Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly.
  • If you fail to repeat a sequence exactly,Simon responds with a "RAZZ" sound. This means you've lost, and the sequence of signals ends.
  • Press any key to restart the game
